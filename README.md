**BovineMatch** is an innovative computer vision project dedicated to bringing rapid, high-accuracy breed classification directly to livestock farmers. We are building the definitive AI tool capable of identifying **73 distinct bovine breeds** (cattle and buffalo) from field images.

### How it Works (The User Experience)

Our goal is simplicity and speed:

1.  **Snap a Picture:** A farmer uses our mobile application to take a photo of an animal in their herd.
2.  **Upload & Match:** The image is uploaded to our powerful AI backend.
3.  **Instant ID:** The system instantly outputs the **exact breed name** from our database of 73 types.

This is all about replacing guesswork with solid data to make herd management faster and smarter.
